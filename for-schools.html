Who this is for

From improving parent engagement to supporting student wellbeing and staff confidence, sessions can be tailored for individual schools, pastoral teams, or trust-wide delivery.

Primary & Secondary Schools

Parent workshops, staff CPD, and pupil sessions designed to strengthen family engagement, emotional wellbeing, and behaviour support — aligned with Ofsted priorities around personal development and behaviour & attitudes.

Multi-Academy Trusts

Trust-wide parenting and family engagement support delivered consistently across settings, with scalable programmes, flexible delivery models, and one experienced delivery partner.

Conferences & Events

Keynote talks and breakout sessions for education conferences, local authority events, and early years or wellbeing summits — tailored to your audience, setting, and objectives.

Programmes & talks

A clear menu of formats — adapted to your setting.

Each programme below is a starting point. Content, depth and delivery are shaped around your specific phase, catchment and priorities.

Parent engagement

"The Calm Home" — parent seminar series

A series of 90-minute sessions for parents on setting boundaries, managing big feelings, and reducing conflict at home. Delivered in your hall or online.

  • Seminar 1: The power of positive parenting
  • Seminar 2: Raising confident, competent children
  • Seminar 3: Raising resilient children
  • Seminar 4: Helping children develop healthy screen-time habits
  • Seminar 1: Raising responsible teenagers
  • Seminar 2: Raising competent teenagers
  • Seminar 3: Getting teenagers connected

Best for: primary and secondary settings wanting to strengthen home-school behaviour consistency.

Staff CPD

Trauma-aware conversations with parents

A practical CPD day for pastoral staff, SENCOs and senior leaders on how to have difficult parent conversations without escalating them.

  • Full INSET day or two twilight sessions
  • Realistic role-play scenarios from your setting
  • Written framework staff can apply the next morning

Best for: schools where parent complaints or escalations are climbing.

Student wellbeing

Year group assembly series

Age-appropriate talks for pupils on emotional regulation, friendships, coping mechanisms and decision making.

  • Single assembly or three-assembly arc
  • Form-time discussion prompts
  • Parent companion note to send home

Best for: schools responding to rising anxiety or peer-conflict referrals.

Keynote

Keynote: "Parents aren't the problem."

A 45-minute signature talk for education and early-years conferences on why genuine parental partnership — not another policy — is the fastest route to student outcomes.

  • 45-minute keynote + 15-minute Q&A
  • Slide deck and speaker notes supplied in advance
  • Can be tailored to your conference theme

Best for: headline slots at MAT conferences, LA events, and sector summits.

Why it matters

Aligned with Ofsted, anchored in evidence.

Every session is designed to support — and be legible to — the frameworks your leadership team already answers to.

  • Grounded in evidence-based practice and aligned with Ofsted priorities around behaviour, wellbeing, parental engagement, and personal development.
  • Based on the Triple P (Positive Parenting Program) — one of the most researched parenting frameworks in the world.
  • Designed to complement, not duplicate your existing SEMH, safeguarding, and pastoral provision.
  • Focused on measurable impact — attendance data, parent feedback, and follow-up metrics where appropriate.
  • Delivered personally by one experienced practitioner with 15+ years of frontline experience, an Enhanced DBS certificate, full insurance, and ongoing supervision.
  • Flexible, scalable pricing for Multi-Academy Trusts and Local Authority commissions, with discounted rates for multi-setting delivery.
