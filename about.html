About Meave

17+ years. Thousands of families. One practitioner

I started this work because I saw too many families being handed leaflets when what they needed was a safe, non-judgemental space to talk, with tried and tested strategies to learn and implement. Today I help parents, schools and organisations transform parenting challenges into parenting successes, without jargon, judgement or a one-size-fits-all script.

Hear from me

From frontline practitioner to independent consultant

Following the closure in 2025, of the charity I led for a decade, I chose to continue the work independently, combining years of leadership experience with a more personal, flexible approach to supporting families.

My route into this work was shaped first and foremost by my experience as a mother of two daughters. That personal insight led me into a role as a Parent Support Advisor in a secondary school and in a local charity in Hillingdon, before eventually setting up my own London-based parenting charity, working alongside local authorities, schools, the prison service and the NHS.

In those years I learned that generic parenting advice rarely survives a Tuesday evening. Real change comes from strategies that families can actually use on the school run, at the dinner table, at bedtime, not in a clinic. That is why I only work with frameworks that have been tested in the real world, primarily the Triple P (Positive Parenting Program), one of the most effective, evidence-based parenting models in the world.

Since going independent, I have been able to focus entirely on the work that makes a difference, 1-to-1 coaching with parents, talks and workshops in schools and strategic consultancy for organisations that want to do parenting support properly.

"Based in London, I work with families and organisations across the UK, both online and in person, and regularly travel for consultancy, training and speaking engagements."

What is Triple P?

The framework behind my work

Triple P (Positive Parenting Program) is an internationally recognised, evidence-based parenting programme that helps parents build positive, confident relationships with their children. Rather than offering a one-size-fits-all approach, Triple P provides practical tools and strategies that can be tailored to each family's needs, helping parents manage challenging behaviour, strengthen family relationships, and create a calmer, more positive home environment.

I have utilised the Triple P principles personally and with thousands of families and found it to be effective and family friendly.

How I work

Evidence-based, non-judgemental, practical

Three principles shape everything I do, with parents in my coaching room, with staff in a school hall, and with boards in an organisation.

Practical strategies backed by evidence

My work is based on evidence-based parenting frameworks such as Triple P, approaches that have been tested with thousands of families and designed to work in real everyday situations, not just in theory.

Warmth without judgement

My approach is never about blame or judgement. Parenting is challenging enough without feeling criticised. I work alongside you to find realistic strategies, strengthen what's already working, and reduce some of the pressure families are carrying.

Practical support with measurable impact

From one-to-one coaching to organisational consultancy, my work is built around clear goals, meaningful progress and outcomes that can genuinely be seen and felt.
