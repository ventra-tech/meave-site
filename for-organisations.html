For Employers

Working parents are 40% of your workforce. Support them properly.

Parental status is the single strongest predictor of whether a mid-career employee stays or leaves. Most employers respond with enhanced leave and a Slack channel. I help you go further — and do it credibly.

  • Manager training on parental transitions (maternity, paternity, return-to-work, bereavement)
  • Lunch-and-learn series on common parenting challenges — anxiety, sleep, neurodiversity, teens
  • 1-to-1 coaching slots as a benefit to working-parent employees
  • Policy and language review — so your parental offer matches your brand
  • Quarterly reporting with engagement, retention and NPS data

Engagement models

Three ways to work with me.

Advisory day

A focused day on-site or online. Diagnostic review of your current parenting-support offer, with a written summary and prioritised recommendations within one week.

Ideal for: leaders exploring whether to invest here at all.

Retained partnership

A rolling quarterly engagement — typically 2–3 days per month — covering programme delivery, manager coaching, and executive reporting.

Ideal for: HR, People or Wellbeing leads with a live remit.

Project engagement

Scoped, time-limited work with a clear deliverable: a commissioned group programme, a policy overhaul, a pathway redesign, a funder-ready evaluation.

Ideal for: charities, LAs and nurseries with a specific mandate.
